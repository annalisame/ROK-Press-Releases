Korea Announces Participation in Six-Party Talks

Republic of Korea
May 13, 2022
The Korean government announces their participation in the upcoming conference and looks forward to working with the delegates from the Russian Federation, Democratic Republic of Korea, People's Republic of China, United States of America, and Japan. Ms. Miulli, the press correspondent for the delegation of the Republic of Korea shares this message to fellow negotiators, "we look forward to the coming negotiations and hope the other delegates are planning on fully participating."

The other members of the delegation are listed below

  • Annalisa Mueller-Eberstein
  • Minsoo Kim
  • Krisha Simon
  • Melanie Miulli
  • Shaela Haga
  • Spencer Goldstein
